About Me

My name is Kiem Vukadinovic, and I have dedicated over eight years to the healthcare field, gaining experience across multiple specialties. As a double board-certified Nurse Practitioner, I specialize in Primary Care, Geriatric Care, Obesity Management, and Aesthetic Medicine.

My passion for aesthetics stems from a deep belief in enhancing natural beauty with artistry and precision. I founded RadiantilyK Aesthetic to provide personalized, confidence-boosting treatments that help my clients feel their best. Witnessing the joy and renewed confidence in my clients fuels my commitment to delivering exceptional care.

Beyond my practice, I am committed to continual learning, attending advanced workshops and educational courses to refine my techniques. I also cherish time with my family, friends, and traveling the world, always seeking inspiration to elevate my craft.

I look forward to meeting you and helping you embrace your most radiant self!
